About Me

Hello! my name is Lisandro Mayancela and I am currently pursing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science as well as a minor in East Asian Studies and Education Studies. My interest in Computer Science actually began at a young age when I fell in love with gaming and wanted to help make games so that I can bring these experiences to the next generation of kids. As I advanced through high school and learned more about CS, my goals shifted to wanting to work on Web/App development. In college, this would once again evolve as I now have a passion for eventually bringing my skills into education so that I can help foster the next generation of PoC students in tech so that the field can be diversified and all students can have an equal opportunity to get involved in CS.

InspiredxSTEM

During my junior year of high school I was a part of planning our school's first hackathon, InspiredxSTEM. During the event itself I served as a mentor to students ranging from middle school to early high school as they were working on their projects.
